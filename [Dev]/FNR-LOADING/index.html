RULES

01. No Intention to Roleplay

          Any activity that demonstrates zero intention to come into the server and roleplay is not allowed. Players are expected to be in character at all times while in the server unless speaking to staff. Initiation is expected by both players to be considered a roleplay scenario.

02. FTVL/Fear RP

          You must value your life and health at ALL times during roleplay and to the best of your ability, value your car, property and possessions. There is no variance of this rule regarding style/character within roleplay.

03. No RDM

          Random Death Match is when you attack/kill someone without RP reason whatsoever and it is not allowed in this server.

04. No VDM

          Vehicle Death Match is when you intentionally use a vehicle to attack/kill another player, without very serious RP reason, and is not allowed in this server.
